Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How accurate is the GPA calculator?

Our GPA calculator uses the standard 4.0 scale and absolute grading system. It accurately calculates your GPA based on the marks and absolute values you enter for each assessment.

Is my data saved securely?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device using browser storage. We don't send your academic data to any external servers, ensuring your privacy.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

GradeRocket is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

How do I reset my progress?

You can clear your data by going to your browser settings and clearing site data, or you can manually remove entries from each subject in the calculator.
